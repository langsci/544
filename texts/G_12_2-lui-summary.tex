Margaret often wanted to reminisce about what a good hunter her husband Lis Osi had been. These days in the forest were something special for the whole family. As well as building up supplies, such days also meant an abundance of food. The meat of the small animals would be eaten directly in the bush camp. She remembers hunting days that took place in the bush section called Diyewi (\textref{sc0202}, Sequence \ref{ex:nrexnapoou}).
