    \ParallelRText{Imelda is sitting above in a \textit{numu}-palm. She is sitting on a palm rib to cut the raw material for making a broom. She is sitting on one rib. She takes only the tops. She cuts off only the tops of the fronds. She takes the tops and scrapes off the layer of the leaves.}
