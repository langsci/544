    \ParallelLText{\textit{Bi \mbox{seleyo} mini. Uke wok ar pi, bi wip ni, arme ni, umeko ni. Uke sû mappe \mbox{seleyo}, bi ar mini. Dop ili onopi nikiyei. Bi dorimaliye. Yala sele kuso ponake. Sele klayepanam. Sû sûli nikiyei. Bi dop ili sû sûli roise nikiyei. Dop ili le. Ono neki. Imiyu neki. Iner dop ili, ono iner ili le.}}
    \ParallelRText{The pigs come to the gardens. When we don't work there, the pigs eat the taro. They eat \textit{arme}-taro and they eat \textit{umeko}-taro. When we light fires in the gardens, the pigs don't come. They smell the body odour of people. Then the pigs turn back to their hidden places. Better one always does the gardens. One must not neglect the gardens. The pigs smell the smoke of fire. They smell the body odour and the smoke. Body odour whiffs. Somebody must be nearby. A sorcerer is around. The body odour of the armpit, the armpit odour of a man hangs in the air.}
