The text describes a contraceptive method (Sequences \ref{ex:nrexsekbop} and \ref{ex:nrexkomarr}), which is similar to using a vaginal diaphragm. The husband must not know that the wife wishes to avoid a pregnancy. The text also mentions a method for becoming pregnant (Sequences \ref{ex:nrexpuluel} and \ref{ex:nrexaeunem}). 
Unfortunately, I was unable to botanically identify the tree, which is said to enhance fertility. Finally, the text speaks of miscarriages (Sequences \ref{ex:nrexsuippu} and \ref{ex:nrexraripw}), after which the fetus was buried in the ground.

In the past, women had to sleep in a special small hut called \textit{pol yip} in Kilmeri during their menstruation. Menstrual blood was collected from a hole in the ground.
