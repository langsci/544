\ea\label{ex:nrexpusulo}
Pudu ikoina pi. Pu suloimoina pi. Pupi pi. Pupi pule, ripap.\\
\gll pu\_du	ikoi-na	pi	pu	suloimoina	pi	pupi	pi	pupi	pule	ripap\\
     rain.darkness	big-\textsc{adv}	\textsc{lv}	rain	huge	\textsc{lv}	wind	\textsc{lv}	wind	come	storm\\
\glt `The darkness of the rainy weather is great. There is a heavy rain. It is windy. Wind comes, a storm.'
